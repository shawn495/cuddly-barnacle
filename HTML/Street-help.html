Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Delivery

Q: How do I place an order?

A: Easy, browse our menu, pick your street flava, and hit “Add to Cart.” When you’re ready, check out online, in-app, or come see us in person.

Q: Do you deliver to my area?

A: Check out our Find a Street page, it’ll show you if we roll into your neighborhood.

Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yup! Just head to Find a Street and hit “Track My Order” to see where your meal is cruising from our kitchen to your door.

Menu & Food

Q: Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

A: Absolutely, we’ve got street bowls, wraps, and plant powered plates from around the globe.

Q: What if I have food allergies?

A: We take it seriously. Ask our team in-store or check the allergen info on each menu item online before ordering.

Q: Can I customise my meal?

A: This is your street, swap, add, or spice it up your way.

Gift Cards & Vouchers

Q: Do you sell gift cards?

A: Yes! Perfect for birthdays, thank-yous, or “just because.” They’re redeemable online, in-store, and on the StreetFlava app.

Q: How can I buy gift cards in bulk?

A: Email us at [orders@streetflava.com](mailto:orders@streetflava.com) and we’ll make it happen.

General

Q: Where are you located?

A: Hit Find a Street, you’ll see all our locations plus opening hours.

Q: Do you cater for events?

A: Oh yeah. From block parties to boardrooms, we bring the flava to you. Drop us a line to get started.

Q: Can I work at StreetFlava?

A: We’re always looking for fresh talent with flava. Keep an eye on our socials for job drops.
